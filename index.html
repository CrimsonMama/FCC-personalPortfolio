About Me

Creativity has been in my family for many generations. When I was younger I enjoyed drawing and painting and had an interest in comic book art. I recieved A-Levels in both Art and Psychology but never really had a strong idea of what I wanted to do as a career. I went to college and got a diploma in Art but my passion was more for my personal interests than what I could do on a career level. I also realised too late that Graphic Design would have been a better choice more suited to the type of art I enjoyed.
After many years spent working and travelling and concentrating on family life I found myself in a dark place. But I learned from my mistakes and found myself with the time and maturity to know what I wanted from my life! For the first time in 20 years I could pursue a career and found a love for computer programming, web design and web development!
Computers are definitely the future! And allows me to combine my creative talents with the technological skills of the world today! And the scope of fields available to work in is vast! I love the knowledge that in this field I will always have more to learn and different areas to explore!

"Computer science today is the great frontier that space exploration once was!"
